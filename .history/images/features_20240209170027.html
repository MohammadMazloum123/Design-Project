The Loruki Platform

At the heart of the ONE is a complex hybrid powertrain that combines a 1.6-liter turbocharged V6 engine with four electric motors. The result is a staggering 1,063 horsepower, propelling the ONE from 0 to 60 mph in just 2.9 seconds and on to a top speed of over 217 mph.
